Youngster science Reading Club

In co-operation with Ipsum public library we have group meetings once a month, and every week we recommend books. Some of our meetings will be held down at Ipsum library, and others will be held here at Community Science Museum. To ensure the books are interestingand engaging we have split the club into two groups. The younger age group is 6 to 12 years, while the older group is 12 years pluss. To apply, please e-mail: lorem_ipsum@ipsum.com

Explore natureBiology Club

Every other monday during summer break, we get out into the great outdoors. we explore, colect samples, have lunch and get back to have a closer look at our samples.

We start at 09:30 and finish at 13:30. Currently we have two groups. One aged 8 - 12, and one group for older kids. There are currently 4 free spots aged 8 - 12, and 1 free spot for the older group.

We start this summer at Jun 30. Each period lasts 4 weeks and costs 90 dollars.

If you are interested, please e-mail: lorem_ipsum@ipsum.com
