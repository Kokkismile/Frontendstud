For youngsters interested inHaving fun with Science

Come on down to our museum, there’s plenty to see and do. You can learn about Newtonian physics from our bumper swing, or why not travel back in time and meet our resident Woolly Mammoth? Our exhibits are designed to be accessible for interested minds, so make sure you come ready to learn and explore.

If none of that peek your interest, our space collection might. No? What about electrical devices with visible sparks, lightening bolts, or dancing robots?

We also have the Young Stars club, which meets on Saturday between 10:00 and 13:00. You’ll get to explore and experiment together with us!

During the school summer break we run the biology club where you can join other children your age to go on a journey of discovery in the great outdoors.

We also have the youngster science reading club were we recommend and review new books every week, and get together once a month.

Sincerely yours, staff
